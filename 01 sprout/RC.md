[[encryption]]

## RC4

Stream cipher using variable key size from `40 - 2048` bits and is used in SSL and WEP

## RC5

Block cipher up to key sizes of `2048` bits

## RC6

Based on [[#RC5]] and was introduced as a replacement for [[DES]]
