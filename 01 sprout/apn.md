**Access point name**

Logical scheme to show what the [[mn]] in entitled to

This includes things like
- Internet access
- HD video streaming
- Content rich gaming etc

Each APN specifies what type of connectivity is enabled on that APN
- IPv6
- IPv4
- IPv4v6
