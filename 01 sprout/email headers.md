# email headers
---
Topics: #email
Created: 2022-12-22 00:55:37

---

Some of the general email headers include

- **From**
- **To**
- **Date**
- **Subject**
- **Return path**
- [[email domain key]]
- [[DKIM]] signatures
- [[SPF]]
- **Message ID**
- **MIME version**


Beyond these ones, we also have some that are specific to vendors or added by them for certain functionality e.g. spam

- **X-headers**
- **X-received**
- **X-Spam**
- **X-mailer**
