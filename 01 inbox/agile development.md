This is like the [[waterfall model]] but on a much shorter time scale

While waterfall might take months, agiles are usually split into sprints 2 - 4 weeks long

1. **Plan**
2. **Design**
3. **Develop**
4. **Testing**
5. **Deployment**
6. **Review**

This takes is much faster
